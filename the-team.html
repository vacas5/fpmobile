FPBRFuturists

With tenures as long as 26 years, FPBR has maintained a consistent management team that have guided Faith's trend principles over four decades. Outside of the management team, we are made up of Sr. and Jr. Strategists, Consultants, Trend analysts, as well as a string support team of finance, design, digital, and IT staff.

Ted Pulton

Principal

At FPBR, Ted has engaged his extensive branding and marketing experience across many challenging client assignments. Each has benefited from deft leadership, insightful judgment and unwavering commitment to A++ success. These include J&J's LifeScan Division (Blood Glucose Meter Innovation), the turn-around of CitiBank, the re-positioning of SC Johnson from "A Family Company" to "A Company for Families," Business and Brand transformation for Humana and WellPoint, and many others. Ted joined Faith Popcorn's BrainReserve from Gotham Incorporated, a leading New York advertising agency, where for 14 years he was Chief Marketing Officer. At Gotham he launched WebMD and E*TRADE, repositioned Priceline.com and grew agency gross revenues from $26 Million to more than $650 Million. Prior to Gotham, Ted was a Partner, EVP and Director of Client Services at New York advertising agency Berenter Greenhouse & Webster. He lives in Connecticut with his wife Laura; his son Devin and daughter Danielle are college and graduate students respectively.

Kate Newlin

Managing Director

Kate Newlin is a Principal at Faith Popcorn's BrainReserve. She is the author of Passion Brands and Shopportunity! Kate is a frequent speaker at retail and marketing events and is often interviewed by trade and national media for her perspectives on the emerging marketspace.
